About Us

Christopher is the owner and founder of Crane Tech Repair. He started this company in 2018. Christopher has been repairing technology for almost two decades. He started when his family was always breaking the computer at home. Christopher has a passion for fixing things, and making sure people have a wonderful experience. He is dedicated to fixng peoples problems for the best price. Christopher grew up in Utah and has a passion for helping others. Him and his Wife Kali love to help those in need. Christopher is Certified by Apple to repair many different devices ranging from MacBook Pro's to iPhones.
